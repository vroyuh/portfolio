Wall Art Whisperer

August – December 2025

Role:

UX Researcher

Overview:

MAJIA was born from my personal experience as a solo traveler. During my first solo trip, I struggled to find resources and accommodations that were tailored to the unique needs of a single traveler. Researching options for lodging, budgeting, and planning often felt overwhelming. I realized these trips could be so much easier with the right resources — so I decided to create MAJIA to help other solo travelers like myself.
