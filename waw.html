Wall Art Whisperer

August – December 2025

Role:

UX Researcher

Overview:

Wall Art Whisperer is a digital spacing web app created for professional art installers/handlers and general users (interior decorators and home users). The goals was to determine whether Wall Art Whisperer meets a real need in the market and identify which user group(s) find it most useful, what features they value most, and what barriers exist to broader adoption.
